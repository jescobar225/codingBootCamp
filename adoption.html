Adoption Partners

Cat Haven is a non-profit organization aimed at rescuing felines in our community and finding them forever homes. They offer adoption of cats ranging from 10 weeks old to elderly, low-cost spaying/neutering, and fostering/volunteering opportunities.

If you're interested in adopting a feline friend please click the to visit Cat Haven's adoption portal.

Companion Animal Alliance is the nonprofit Baton Rouge animal shelter formed in 2010 to increase the save rate of animals in our community. In partnership with the East Baton Rouge City-Parish, CAA operates the EBR Parish open-intake shelter, caring for nearly 8,000 animals each year including cats, dogs, horses, wildlife, and exotic animals.

Although they take care of many different species of animals, only dogs and cats are available for adoption at the moment. Click the link to visit their adoption page

In 2005 Spay Baton Rouge was born to help make spaying and neutering affordable to pet owners who otherwise couldn't afford this important but often cost prohibitive procedure for their pets. Since then, the organization has helped to spay or neuter over 15,600 cats and dogs in the Baton Rouge area, including both people's owned pets and stray, feral, and community animals.

If you are interested in learning more about this program or applying for their services, please click the link to head over to Spay Baton Rouge.

There are many benefits to adopting a pet, but a great one is that many rescues/shelters offer services at a lower cost or included in your adoption fee which you would otherwise have to pay for separately at a veterinarian on your first visit with your new pet.

Our partners offer the following services included in their adoption fees:

  • Spay/Neuter
  • Testing for diseases
  • De-flea/de-worming treaments
  • Microchipping
  • Age-appropriate vaccinations

These services can cost over $400 at certain veterinarian clinics, so our partners make it very affordable to adopt by including these services in your adoption fees.
